Audio Environment Survey

This survey is intended to explore what kinds of sounds foster wellbeing and productivity. Please press "play" and have this survey playing in the background as you work. Adjust the levels of the different sounds to find the mix that you find most pleasant and helpful to your work.

Feel free to take as much time as you would like to find your ideal balance. Once you have finished your work, please fill out the questions at the bottom of the survey and hit the "submit" button to submit your mix and your responses. Thank you for your participation.
