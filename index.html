Please have this survey playing in the background as you work. Sound sliders start all the way down. Adjust the levels of the different sounds to find the mix that you find most pleasant and helpful to your work.

Feel free to take as much time as you would like to find your ideal balance. Once you have finished your work, please fill out the questions at the bottom of the survey and hit the "submit" button to submit your mix and your responses. Thank you for your participation.
